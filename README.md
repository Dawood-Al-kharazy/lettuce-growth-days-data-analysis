## Defining the big-picture goal
**The business problem:** There is a slowdown in lettuce growth in August and September.

**The goal:** Define the main factors that caused the slowdown in the lettuce growth process, and find out the most efficient three recommendations.

**Data sources:** https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/jurijsruko/lettuce/data

## Formulate SMART questions
1. How did temperature levels in August affect the crop growth period?
2. If crops were grown at a high level of humidity, how many more days would the crops grow compared to the number of days if the crops were grown at a low level of humidity?
3. If the period of planting crops is on sunny days, will there be a decrease in the number of growing days by more than 5 days, compared to cloudy days?
4. Is there any relationship between pH levels higher than 6.2 and crops growing more than 5 days slower?

## Perform a Data Reality Check

### Purpose
This project aims to define the main factors that caused the slowdown of the lettuce growth process in Farmer Hassan's farm in August and September by monitoring the surrounding factors of the lettuce (Temperature, Humidity, pH levels, and TDS Value) in August and September that were collected through sensors on the farm. In this project, we will analyze the data to identify the most effective factor causing the slowdown in the growth process, and we will recommend the three most effective solutions to accelerate the growth process by at least 5 days.

### Scope / Major Project Activities

| Activity | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| Data collection | Collect (Temperature, Humidity, pH levels, and TDS Value) data from the farm's sensors. |
| Define the main factors that caused the slowdown of the lettuce growth | Analyze the farm's data to identify the biggest factor. |
| Create accelerated growth recommendations | Create recommendations to accelerate the growth of lettuce, estimate the number of days that each recommendation can shorten, and rank these recommendations by the number of days. |
| Deliver the final report | Deliver the final report of factors and recommendations to Hussain. |

### This project does not include:
* The data from the months except August and September
* Any plant except lettuce
* Implementing any solutions or recommendations
